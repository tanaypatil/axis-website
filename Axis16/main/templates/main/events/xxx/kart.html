“We make living by what we get but we make life by what we give.”

We all have in one way or the other benefitted from the society, so it’s time to give something back.
If you feel so, then you have found your way and if not, Kartavya is a right place to start with
KARTAVYA, brings with it the real problems faced by NGOs and several communities in the form of a
problem statement. It would challenge your intellect and make you think out of the box to win it.
The winners of KARTAVYA’16 will have the opportunity to work as a social intern abroad .

This event would consist of two rounds:

QUALIFIER ROUND
In this particular round,the participants would be given a problem statement and they will have to
submit an abstract based on it.These would be problems faced by ngo’s. Round 1 consists of two parts :

This problem satement has to be answered in maximum 1000 words . The solution should be more practical
and be applicable in real life scenario . Also mention your name, college and department in the abstract.

FINAL ROUND
These shortlisted candidates would then have to do case study on the main problem statement and
prepare a presentation which would be presented to the panal. The winner would be selected based on
the points scored.

Part A:
India’s most of the population lives in slums and are deprived of nutrition, education and a descent culture. In this pool of poverty and innocence grows a worm of addiction of children to tabacoo, liquor and smoking habits. These things at such a soft tender age rings an alarm in our society. Write few ways how the children below 14 years can be protected from such a disastrous addiction in their slums? Suggest ways about how you can change their livelihood .
