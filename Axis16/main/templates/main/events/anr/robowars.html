Qualifying Round

  1. This will be a knockout round. Match length : 5 mins.
  2. The main aim should be to destroy or immobilise the opponent bot.
  3. If none of the bots are destroyed during the match then the points structure ,that will be mentioned, will be followed.
  4. The teams qualifying this round reaches the semifinal round.

Semifinal Round

  1. There will be two rounds of 3 mins each.
  2. The team that destroys the opponent team completely or which wins according to the points accumulated
    in both the rounds finds way to the GRAND FINALS.

Robot Specifications
  1. Max permissible dimensions 65x65x85 cm at the starting of the match.
  2. Max permissible weight of the robot:35-40 Kg (excluding power supply).
  3. Cylinders for Pneumatics are excluded from the weight of the robot,
    if and only if they are kept outside the arena at all times. Such robots are not eligible for wireless bonus.
  4. Cylinder pressure limit for pneumatics is 5 bar.
  5. 36 volts between any two points in the circuit.
  6. Wireless robots will have an advantage in case of a tie.
  7. Use of Pneumatics is permitted.
  8. Any arrangement made only for hoisting the cables is not counted in maximum height limit.
  9. Participants must provide means(nozzle) to measure the cylinder pressure.
  10. All bot should have easily visible and controlled mobility in order to compete.
  11. COMPRESSER in any case will not be allowed.
  12. The event managers will have a final say in all the matters which occur during the event

Arena Specifications
Dimension of the arena is 12x12 ft.

1. Wireless Robot should ensure zero interference.
2. Do not use any tether cables, projectiles, nets.
3. No chemical, inflammable or incendiary weapons are allowed.
4. No liquids are allowed.
5. Robots should not employ any RF jammers or any other similar equipment.
6. If any robot tries to or damages the arena the robot will be disqualified.
7. The event managers will have a final say in all the matters which occur during the event.
