It is the period of civil war!!!

During the battle between the Rebels led by the Jedi Knights and the Evil Galactic Empire led by the Sith Lord Darth Vader who plots to build the empire’s ultimate weapon “DEATH STAR”, an armored space station with enough power to destroy the entire galaxy spies tried to get a route map to the energy source of the Death Star.

Be a Skywalker, a Jedi Knight assigned with a droid (R2D2) help the Resistance destroy the Death Star and bring peace to the galaxy.

Now your task!!
Imagine yourself to be a Skywalker and direct your droid (bot) to reach the main energy systems of the Death Star and destroy the energy source of the Death Star so that it spoils the dreadful plan of Darth Vader.

MAY THE FORCE BE WITH YOU.

The basic problem statement goes in this way.

Build a manually controlled robot which is capable of grabbing different objects and crossing different types of terrain by reconstructing its path and broken bridge.

Round 1

  1. Bot will start from the ground and climb thr slotted bridge which is inclinedat 25 degree to the ground.
  2. While crossing the horizontal bridge, bot should pick up the key(Cylindrical pipe 1.5" inch diameter) and put it
    on the vertical rod which will be at some distance from first, which will start the button for lift.
  3. Further the bot has to go up using the lift which will be controlled by your team mate.
  4. After reaching top position, bot should cross another bridge which will be made of two parallel rods ,
    which can be adjusted according to the distance between the wheels of your bot.
  5. After that you have to cross the hinged bridge.
  6. Further ,put two yellow cube on pyramid and other three rod and yellow cube into the box.
  7. Push the box down, which complete further bridge by one rope pulley mechanism.
  8. The bot will take the cylindrical pipe (1.5"inch diameter) crossing the bridge and collect it in the container.
    More the cylinders more will be the points.
  9. Push the container, along the rod way provided which will end your first round.
