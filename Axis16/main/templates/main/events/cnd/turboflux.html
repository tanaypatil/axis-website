Remember Shah Rukh Khan’s movie SWADES which received an overwhelmingly strong critical acclaim around the globe where he sets up a small hydro-electric power plant that would solve the problem of irregular electricity and make the village self-sufficient. That’s what define TURBOFLUX. Time to bring out the Bernoulli in you and experience your Eureka! moment at AXIS’16. Use your hydraulics knowledge to rotate a turbine by making water pass through several obstacles. Would you prefer a V-shaped or a U-shaped pipe for the flow of water? Which path will make the water fall with the greatest speed? Physics was never this fun!

Participants have to take the water to a certain height using suction pumps (arena is shown in the fig.) overcoming obstacles in the path and various hurdles, as in water reaches the highest level with minimum losses. To measure the energy possessed with the water, it is gushed on to the turbine which is coupled to a dynamo and the voltage generated is measured.

The obsctales will be disclosed on the day of the event and the sequence of the obsctacles will be decided by the participants.

  • Maximum Number of participants per team : 3
  • Participants should come with a turbine.

  • Turbine specifications:
    1. Diameter of turbine should not be more than 80 cm
    2. Bore diameter should be 6 mm.
    3. There is no restriction regarding type of material you use for making turbine.
    4. Weight of turbine should not be more than 1.5 kg.

  • The turbine should be strictly to the specification we have provided. Deviation from it may cause disqualification.
  • The arrangement of the obsctacles and the pumps on the arena as well as mounting of the turbine will be done by the
    Turbo Flux organisers.
  • The participants will be allowed to control the time at which they want the pumps to be switched on.
  • Once the sequence of obstacles decided, no changes will be allowed.
  • Points will be there for using lesser number of pumps than allowed.

• Time water takes from start to reach to turbine.
• Voltage generated due to rotation of turbine.
• Meanwhile, other minor criteria will be revealed that day itself.
• The team with the most innovative turbine will be given extra points.

  1. Is it chemical engineers event?
    No, it is event for engineers of every branch. All you need to do is apply simple fluid mechanics concept studied in school.
  2. Is there any registration fees?
    No, there is no registration fees. The only money that you have to spend is on making your own turbine.
  3. When do we get to know about the obstacles and what are its pre-requisite?
    On the day of event all the obstacles will be disclosed and then you have to decide how you have to
    arrange your obstacles. You just have to come with your turbine.
  4. Is there any guidance regarding making of turbine?
    Yes, a workshop is held few days before the event to give you an idea regarding what are the various
    methods by which we can make turbine from daily use items and cost efficient.
