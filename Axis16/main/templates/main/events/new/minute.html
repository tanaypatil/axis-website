Initiating an all new contest "ELECTROBLITZ" that will test you to put together the jigsaw puzzle of electrical components and logic gates, one at a time to create a big picture!
Are you the next Tesla, Faraday or Volta? Is your room a junkyard of transistors, and nose habituated to the burning smell of solder? Do you have a proclivity to debug electronic circuits and design new ones? Then here’s your chance to showcase your electroholic skills and win cool prizes.

ELECTROBLITZ is a technical event in which your knowledge about basic electrical and electronics will be tested on the basis of 3 rounds. So brush up your skills and get ready to face the challenge of “THE GAME OF CIRCUITS”.

  • Round 1
    This Round will require all the participants individually to give a pen-paper test consisting of MCQ’s and
    subjective questions. Maximum time limit is 30 min. The questions will be asked on basic electrical and
    digital & analog electronics. The test will be held on 24th September 2016 at VNIT and will begin sharply
    at 10 a.m.

    The test will consist of 15 MCQ’s of 2 marks each and 5 subjective questions of 6 marks each.
    There will be negative marking of -1 for each wrong answer in MCQ’s. No negative marking for subjective section.

  • Round 2
    Based on performance in Round 1, the shortlisted participants will be called for Round 2 to be held on the
    same day. In this Round participants will have to form groups of 2 members each. Herein, the groups will
    be evaluated based on their fault detecting skills. They will be given a number of circuit diagrams which are
    not producing the expected output. The groups are required to detect the faults in each circuit and draw
    the correct circuit diagram on paper to obtain the correct output. Time limit is 1 hour. Shortlisted Groups will
    move to the final round.
