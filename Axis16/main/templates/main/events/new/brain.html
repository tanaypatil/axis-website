A Nagpur citywide quiz contest tailored specifically for all the quizzing enthusiasts out there. Brainstorm not only incorporates the time honoured General Knowledge and Current Affairs topics, but also includes questions on technology, in keeping up with the heart and soul of a techfest. Let’s see if you have the IQ to crack the AQ (AXIS Quiz!) , the Orange City’s first ever TechQuiz!

Brainstorm consists of two levels.

  • Level 1 is a Nagpur city-wide pen and paper round, comprising of 20 general knowledge and current affairs based questions to be solved in a time duration of 30 minutes. Every team consists of 2 participants. The top winning team from every participating college of Level 1 will be invited for Level 2 at the VNIT Campus during AXIS’16.

  • Level 2 will challenge quizzers in various fields through various interactive and innovative rounds. So, gear up and be ready to brainstorm!
