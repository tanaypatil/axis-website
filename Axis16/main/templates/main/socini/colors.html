Colors Of Nagpur

Every festival has darker shades to it, an underbelly that is not always visible to the eye. So, is the case with Holi, the festival of colours. AXIS’16, conducted a heart-warming city-wide series called Colours of Nagpur, wherein we brought to light the stories of those who go unnoticed, unheeded working relentlessly as the rest of the city was revelling in the spirit of Holi. Nurses, petrol pump workers, traffic policemen, airport staff, the ‘Coolie’ at the train station, we covered them all. Just a simple act of being a good patient listener showed that we cared.
