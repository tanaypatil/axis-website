World Environment Day

Does it sadden you to watch trees being felled only to pave the way to concrete jungles? If it does, the My Tree, My Friend initiative by AXIS’16 was just the place for you to have been. ‘ My Tree, My Friend’ was a Nagpur city-wide attempt to open the Nagpurian’s eyes as to how their once green, tree lined city was fast urbanizing into yet another grey, polluted, metropolis. Following on the lines of the friendship day trend of tying ribbons to one’s friends, the students of VNIT instead each befriended a tree by tying a green ribbon around it. The green ribbon, signified a promise of protection in the face of deforestation, from the younger generation to the good old trees whose shade they had grown up under.
