Make A Difference

Making a difference in the lives of those who have not been as fortunate as ourselves in society is what AXIS Social Initiatives strive for. On this world literacy day, AXIS’16 in association with the youth run NGO, Make A Difference brought a smile to the faces of many young children by introducing them to the world of Sci-Fi. With a range of simple science experiments on display and the screening of Wall-E, the animated Sci-Fi movie for children, all in all it was a memorable day for AXIS’16.
